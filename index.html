Facts About Arizona

  1. Arizona was founded February 14, 1912
  2. The capital of Arizona is Phoenix
  3. The state motto is Ditat Deus (God Enriches)
  4. The state bird is the Cactus Wren and the state flower is the Saguaro cactus bloom
