Places to stay in Arizona

Arizona has so many beautiful places to stay but here are just a few to start with.

Phoenix

Phoenix is the Capital Arizona and the 5th largest city in the US

Hotels to stay at in Phoenix:

  • Arizona Grand Resort & Spa
  • Hilton Garden Inn Phoenix
  • Homewood Suites by Hilton

Sedona

Sedona is magical with its beautiful red rocks that will take your breath away

Hotels to stay at in Sedona:

  • Los Abrigados Resort & Spa
  • Hilton Sedona Resort at Bell Rock
  • Arabella Hotel Sedona

Flagstaff

If you want to visit Arizona but break away from the desert landscape Flagstaff is the perfect place to stay

Hotels to stay at in Flagstaff:

  • Hyatt Place Flagstaff
  • Aiden by Best Western Flagstff
  • Double Tree by Hilton Hotel Flagstff
